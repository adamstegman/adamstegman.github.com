Water Wars

Water Consumption Tracking and Competition

deployed

Started on September 8, 2010

Open source. Code available here.

Water Wars is a web application I wrote in Ruby on Rails to track water consumption for my wife and me.

It allows us to see how the other is doing that day at a glance, while quickly adding our own consumption.

We can also compete on weekly totals.
